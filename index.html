HOW TO ENJOY ST. PATRICK’S DAY IN NYC

Having fun and going around the city on St. Patrick’s Day is a must. There is no other city in the USA that celebrates this holiday this way. Of course, there is more to it than simply wearing a green shirt. So, if you like partying, there are some proposals on how to enjoy St.Patrick’s Day in NYC.

Go to the St. Patrick’s Day parade

This celebration starts every year on March 17 in Manhattan. The parade is one of the largest in the world- there are over 200,000 marchers and 2 million spectators. It begins at 44th Street, then the people march past St. Patrick’s Cathedral and finish the parade near the American Irish Historical Society at 79th Street.

One of the ways in which you can celebrate the St. Patrick’s Day in NYC is by going to the parade

Precisely because this is a popular event, it is not allowed to bring any alcohol, even if you are coming just to watch. Celebrate St.Patrick’s Day in NYC in a pub

On this day, there will be a number of deals and events in over 50 bars. You will have a wide selection to choose from. However, you should be aware that you will have to register. This is quite a simple process. First of all, you should visit the website of the Official St. Patrick’s Day Luck of the Irish Pub Crawl. All you should do is to buy tickets online and arrive at your chosen bar. Once you get there, you will meet the staff and get a wristband, a map of participating venues and the list of drink specials. Since this journey can bring you to different neighborhoods, feel free to explore all the areas!

Pay the Merchant’s House Museum a visit

This is the city’s one and only preserved home from the 19th century. It is located in the 4th Street in the neighborhood of NoHo and you should pay special attention when looking for it. The exterior is quite simple and quaint and you would surely walk right by it without knowing what it is.
